About


Physics Blog is a free website for all those who want to gain little knowledge in the field of Physics. Benjamin Franklin said, "An investment in knowledge pays the best interest". With this motive, this website was started to provide free knowledge for everybody. Blogs present on this website is generally written in such a way that a reader from a non-science background will also able to understand.

Mostly the information present in the blogs is generally taken from some other sources like Wikipedia, Quanta Magazine and other physics blogs.

Thank you for visiting the Physics Blog. I invite you to explore our pages.
